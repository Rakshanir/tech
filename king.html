ABOUT KCE

Kings College of Engineering (KCE) was founded in the year 2001 by Raj Educational Trust (RET), Chennai, with the singular aim of providing quality technical education to the economically disadvantaged and rural community. KCE is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. KCE, a NAAC accredited institution, is conferred AUTONOMOUS STATUS by UGC, New Delhi, and Anna University, Chennai.
